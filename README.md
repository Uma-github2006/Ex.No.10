# Ex.No.10 : Content Creation (Reports, Articles, Case Studies, etc.) Using Prompt Patterns

## Date: 17-11-2025
## Reg. No : 212223060293

## Aim
To demonstrate how various prompting techniques such as query decomposition, decision-making, semantic filtering, and tail generation can be applied to create structured content like reports, articles, and case studies using AI models.
The goal is to show how different prompt structures influence the content’s quality, coherence, and organization.

## Procedure
## Step 1 : Introduction to Prompt Patterns

- Before generating the content, different prompting techniques are studied:

- Prompt Techniques Used

- Query Decomposition: Break a complex topic into smaller sections.

- Decision Making: Guide the model to choose between strategic options.

- Answer Engineering: Provide instructions on structure, tone, and format.

- Fact Check List: Ensure real-world accuracy.

- Tail Generation: Extend content meaningfully for depth.

- Menu Actions: Present options and let AI choose the best direction.

- Semantic Filtering: Control tone (formal, academic, creative, etc.).

## Step 2 : Choosing the Content Type

For this experiment, the content selected is:

## Case Study on Business Success — “The Rise of Nykaa”

This case study focuses on how Nykaa, founded by Falguni Nayar, became India’s leading online beauty and wellness marketplace using digital-first strategies, strong branding, and customer-centric innovation.

## Step 3 : Creating the Prompts
## Basic Prompt and Output
## Prompt:

“Write a short case study on how Nykaa became successful.”

## Output:

Nykaa, founded in 2012 by Falguni Nayar, started as an online beauty platform offering premium cosmetics and personal care products.
The company focused on authenticity, a wide product range, and strong partnerships with global brands.
Nykaa’s success came from its omni-channel model, influencer-driven marketing, and reliable customer experience.
By 2021, Nykaa became one of India’s most trusted beauty marketplaces and launched a successful IPO.

## Step 4 : Refined Prompt and Output (Using Prompt Patterns)
## Prompt:

“Create a detailed business case study on the success of Nykaa using the following structure:
- Introduction

- Problem Faced

- Strategic Decisions (use decision-making prompt)

- Role of Technology (use query decomposition)

- Marketing and Customer Engagement (use tail generation)

- Results and Impact

- Conclusion — apply semantic filtering for professional tone.”

## Output:

## Case Study: The Rise of Nykaa — A Digital Beauty Revolution

## 1. Introduction

- Nykaa, launched in 2012 by former investment banker Falguni Nayar, has reshaped India’s beauty and wellness market.
- What began as an online beauty store grew into a trusted omni-channel brand with millions of active users.

## 2. Problem Faced

- At the time of launch, India lacked a reliable online platform for authentic beauty products.
- Consumers faced issues such as counterfeit items, limited brand availability, and poor product guidance.

## 3. Strategic Decisions (Decision-Making Prompt)

- Nykaa made several crucial decisions:

- Adopted an inventory-led model instead of a marketplace to ensure authenticity.

- Expanded from online-only to Nykaa Luxe and Nykaa On Trend physical stores.

- Created its own private-label beauty products.

- Invested heavily in influencer partnerships through “Nykaa Army.”

- These decisions strengthened brand trust and market presence.

## 4. Role of Technology (Query Decomposition)

- Nykaa used technology strategically by breaking down business challenges:

- Product Discovery: AI-based personalized recommendations.

- Customer Education: Video tutorials and virtual try-on tools.

- Inventory Management: Real-time stock tracking across warehouses.

- Data Analytics: Understanding customer buying patterns and trends.

- This improved customer experience and operational efficiency.

## 5. Marketing and Customer Engagement (Tail Generation)

- Nykaa’s marketing success came from:

- Influencer-driven promotions on YouTube and Instagram.

- “Hot Pink Sale,” one of India’s largest beauty discount events.

- Strong content through Nykaa Beauty Book and personalized newsletters.

- Regional language outreach to connect with Tier 2 and Tier 3 customers.

- These strategies helped Nykaa build a loyal community.

## 6. Results and Impact

- Over 5 million active customers by 2021.

- Presence in 90+ cities with 120+ physical stores.

- Successful IPO in 2021, making Falguni Nayar India’s richest self-made woman.

- Transformed India’s beauty market with genuine products and digital-first shopping.

## 7. Conclusion (Semantic Filtering)

Nykaa’s rise represents the power of digital innovation, customer trust, and strategic decision-making. By combining technology, strong branding, and omni-channel presence, Nykaa became a leading force in India’s beauty industry.

## Step 5 : Further Prompt Refinement Example (Answer Engineering + Fact Check List)
## Prompt:

“Rewrite the above case study in a formal business report format with bullet points, factual accuracy, and a professional tone.”

## Output:
## Business Report: Success Story of Nykaa
### Executive Summary

- Founded: 2012

- Founder: Falguni Nayar

- Industry: Beauty, Wellness, E-commerce

- Business Model: Inventory-led + Omni-channel

### Key Success Factors

- Authentic and premium beauty product availability

- Strong influencer marketing

- AI-driven personalization

- Nationwide offline expansion

### Impact

- Created large-scale employment in warehouses and retail

- Set new standards for authenticity in beauty e-commerce

- Emerged as India’s first women-led unicorn startup

## Final Version (Polished Case Study)
## Prompt:

“Generate a final polished version of the Nykaa case study in a formal business tone for academic submission.”

## Output:

Nykaa’s transformation from a small online beauty store to a market-leading omni-channel brand is a strong example of digital innovation in India.Through strategic decisions, technology adoption, influencer marketing, and customer-focused initiatives, Nykaa reshaped the beauty retail landscape.Its journey highlights how consistency, authenticity, and data-driven strategy can drive long-term business success.

## Conclusion

Using advanced prompting techniques such as decision-making, query decomposition, semantic filtering, and tail generation, high-quality business content can be efficiently created.This experiment shows that the structure of the prompt directly influences content accuracy, clarity, and coherence, proving the effectiveness of prompt engineering in academic and business content creation.
